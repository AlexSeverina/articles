\section{two brs}

Let the input grammar is 
\begin{align*}
S & \rightarrow a \ S \ b \ 
\\
S & \rightarrow c \ S \ d \
\\
S & \rightarrow a \ b
\\
S & \rightarrow c \ d
\end{align*}

The input grammar in CNF is 
\begin{align*}
S   & \rightarrow A \ S_1
\\
S_1 & \rightarrow S \ B
\\
S   & \rightarrow C \ S_2
\\
S_2 & \rightarrow S \ D
\\
S   & \rightarrow C \ D
\\
S   & \rightarrow A \ B
\\
C   & \rightarrow c
\\
D   & \rightarrow d
\\
A   & \rightarrow a
\\
B   & \rightarrow b
\end{align*}
